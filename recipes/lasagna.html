Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is how you make a delicious lasagna! An Italian dish made with big flat pieces of pasta with delicious bolognese and creamy cheese sauces between

Ingredients

  • Bolognese sauce
  • Pasta
  • Creamy cheese sauce

Steps

  1. Get a pan and grease it with olive oil
  2. Put a thin layer of bolognese on the bottom
  3. Layer a piece of pasta on top, then more bolognese, then a layer of the creamy cheese sauce
  4. Repeat until you are out of ingredients, finishing with the creamy cheese on top
  5. Put in the preheated oven for 30 minutes uncovered
